Each membership record for a managed group is considered to be "Verified" if the following are true:

  1. Each account member of this group in the app is also a "member" in AnVIL.
  2. Each account admin of this group in the app is also an "admin" in AnVIL.
  3. Each group that is a member of this group in the app is also a "member" in AnVIL.
  4. Each group that is an admin of this group in the app is also an "admin" in AnVIL.

Otherwise, one or more errors will be reported for that membership in the "Errors" table.

The audit also checks for any "members" or "admin" of the group on AnVIL that are not recorded in the app. If any exist, they will be reported in the "Not in app" section.

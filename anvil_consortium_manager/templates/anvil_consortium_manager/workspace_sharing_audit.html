Each sharing record for a managed workspace is considered to be "Verified" if the following are true:

  • The workspace is shared with the group on AnVIL.
  • The group that the workspace is shared with in the app has the same access in AnVIL.
  • The "can_compute" value is the same in the app and on AnVIL.
  • The "can_share" value is the same in the app and on AnVIL.

Otherwise, one or more errors will be reported for that membership in the "Errors" table.

The audit also checks for any group or account that has access and is not recorded in the app. If any exist, they will be reported in the "Not in app" section.
